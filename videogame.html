Pools of the Deep VR - gameplay teaser

A liminal adventure explorer.

Pools of the Deep VR is not your typical game. It is inspired in the new wave of liminal explorers with an added adventure element. In this game you will not die. Take things at your own pace and explore the environments. This is a gameplay teaser of one level. There will be around 6 levels with different styles. The first stage will launch on Steam VR.
