Authenticate app

An education animation to show how to use the Authenticate app and validate SKF products.

  • Art direction - Planning the style, the mood, the story to be told.
  • 3D Modeling - Creation of the products in C4D.
  • 3D animation and rendering - using Unity engine, all the animation were done inside, together with all the lighting and all takes.
  • Video editing - using Premiere, gathering all the takes and screen recordings. Adapting the music.
