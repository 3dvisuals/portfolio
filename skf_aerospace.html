Aerospace

A comprehensive animation to showcase SKF aerospace products, Shown at the Paris Airshow 2023.

The process

This one was one of the most complex projects in my portfolio. We had a large selection of products to place in a long animation that needed to capture the "excitement" of flight. After sourcing generic aircraft models, we proceeded with the creation of the animation. Thanks to Unity the creation process was actually quite fun as we tweaked the ocean, clouds, camera takes and plan where to show our products. The second part involved creating an interactive version, where you click on the hotspots and a small animation takes place. This type of production was used at the Paris Airshow 2023.

  • Art direction - with storyboard and visualization style planning with an intro, a message, a climax and an outro. The message and text were planned together with the marketing manager for that area. 
  • 3D modeling - using C4D and the Step files.
  • 3D animation and rendering - using Unity engine, all the animation were done inside, together with all the lighting and all takes.
  • Graphic design - text animation and fonts, together with icons as per guidelines.
  • Video editing - using Premiere, gathering all the takes. Adapting the music.
