Anti roll fins

A comprehensive animation to showcase SKF stabilizer fins.

The process

From the very beginning, this was an extremely interesting project to create. The first challenge was to able to understand the mechanics behind these incredible products and to translate them into a real time animation. Furthermore, we do not focus only on the product itself but also on its benefits for its users. For this we contextualized and shown how the products perform in action. I am very grateful I was given the opportunity to participate in such a large project with an amazing team, world class.

  • Art direction - with storyboard and visualization style planning with an intro, a message, a climax and an outro. The message and text were planned together with the marketing manager for that area. 
  • 3D modeling - using C4D. Model sourcing from various sites and Step files.
  • 3D animation and rendering - using Unity engine, all the animation were done inside, together with all the lighting and all takes.
  • Graphic design - text animation and fonts, together with icons as per guidelines.
  • Video editing - using Premiere, gathering all the takes. Adapting the music.
